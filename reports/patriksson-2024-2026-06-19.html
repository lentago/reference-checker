Section 6: Forensic Appendix

Methodology

This audit applied the Forensic Reference-Integrity Auditor v5 protocol to the 26-reference list submitted with Patriksson et al. (2024), operating in Mode A (reference list only). This is the third audit pass against this reference list; the prior two passes identified fabricated author-list errors in an earlier draft, all of which have been corrected. The present audit verified each reference independently without assuming prior corrections were complete. All 21 DOI-bearing references were verified via live web search against Crossref (DOI resolution and metadata cross-check), PubMed/PMC (biomedical citation records), and publisher landing pages (BioMed Central, Elsevier/ScienceDirect, Wiley Online Library, Frontiers, PLOS ONE, SAGE Journals, Taylor & Francis). Five references without DOIs (refs 2, 3, 5, 11, 19, 25) were verified via source organization websites, NCBI Bookshelf, EUR-Lex, and secondary citation databases. Retraction Watch was searched for all primary study authors; no retractions were identified. Eight heuristics (H1–H7, H9) were applied to every reference; Heuristic 8 (sneaked reference) is unavailable in Mode A. No submission date was present in the reference file, so the post-submission sub-check of H9 was not applicable; all other H9 checks (journal founding date, volume/issue plausibility) were performed. Risk tier assignments follow the v5 criteria; the integrity score formula is: 100 − (H×12) − (E×5) − (M×2) − (D×3).
