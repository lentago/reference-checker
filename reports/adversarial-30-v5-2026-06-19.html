Audit Limitations

  • Grey-literature references without URLs cannot be independently verified beyond source organization plausibility checks.
  • Shadow-paper detection (H7) depends on database coverage; very recent fabrications may not yet appear in indexed sources.
  • Sneaked-reference detection (H8) was not applied in this Mode A run.
  • Temporal impossibility checks (H9) depend on journal founding-date data in Crossref and the NLM Catalog; coverage may be incomplete for smaller, newer, or recently renamed journals.
  • This audit is a forensic tool, not a definitive determination of fraud. Findings should inform editorial judgment, not replace it.
