Journal Verification — Key Findings

  • Nursing for Women's Health: Launched 2007 as the renamed successor to AWHONN Lifelines (published 1997–2006 under ISSN 1091-5923). Crossref journal metadata confirms earliest NWH DOIs from 2007. A 2004 citation to "Nursing for Women's Health" is temporally impossible under that title. Note: AWHONN Lifelines V8 was published in 2004 — the correct predecessor title for that year.
  • JOGNN (ISSN 0884-2175): Founded 1972, V1=1972. Volume increments by 1 per year. V52 = 2023 (confirmed by web search: JOGNN V52, Issue 4, July 2023). V61 would be published in approximately 2032. Citing V61 in 2023 is impossible — 9 volumes ahead of the actual publication.

Audit Limitations

  • H9 founding-date data depends on Crossref and NLM Catalog coverage; smaller or recently renamed journals may have incomplete records.
  • The post-submission sub-check (b) requires a parsed submission date. In bare Mode A runs without a date header, this sub-check is silently skipped — a reference with a post-submission year will not be flagged unless it also fails another sub-check or another heuristic.
  • Ahead-of-print exemption in sub-check (b): if a 2025 paper cited in a 2024 submission were a registered ahead-of-print article with a confirmed DOI, it would not flag. This test set's ref 4 has a 404 DOI, so the exemption cannot apply.
